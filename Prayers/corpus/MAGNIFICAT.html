    1.

    The Magnificat or Canticle of Mary is one of the prayers used in the Divine Office whose text is derived from the New Testament (Luke 1:46-55). The episode recounts the Visitation, that is Mary’s visit to her cousin Elisabeth, at that time pregnant with John the Baptist, following the events of the Annunciation (archangel Gabriel’s visit to Mary). The canticle itslef is Mary’s answer to Elisabeth’s greeting, which is the source of the Ave Maria.

    In prayer books, the Magnificat can be found within the Vespers, most often in the Hours of the Virign.
