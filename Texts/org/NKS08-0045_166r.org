** Text

[[red 3][O]] hilghe here su(n)te erasmus

hilge bisscop vnde mar-

telare cristi Also du dy gode

offerdest an deme sondaghe

Also nym myn offer to der sele

myner salicheit vnde mynes

lyues Bidde vn(de) man ghot
