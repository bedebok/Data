** Text

[[red 2][S]]ote here ihesu (christ)e Wente du alre

gnaden eyn borne bist vn(de) dyner

gadens neyn gelik Wente se is grot

vn(de) vnbegripelik leue here dor dyner

groten gude ere Help my to aller

tiid vte alle myner noet AmeN
